With De-Tech

De-TECH is an Intrusion Detection System (IDS) that adopts an Anomaly-based Detection approach. Its primary purpose is to identify abnormal behavior or anomalies occurring on a host or network, allowing it to effectively detect emerging forms of attacks. De-TECH is also categorized under Network Behavior Analysis (NBA), which involves passively monitoring incoming and outgoing network traffic over a specific period to establish a baseline of normal traffic patterns.
